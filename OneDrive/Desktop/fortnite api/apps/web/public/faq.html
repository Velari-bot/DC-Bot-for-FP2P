Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PathGen v2

üí¨ Usage Limits

What are the message limits for the Free tier?
Free Tier: 5 messages per day with the AI coach. Your limit resets every 24 hours at midnight UTC.
What are the limits for Pro users?
Pro Tier ($6.99/month):
  • 200 messages per month with the AI coach
  • Chat history saved
  • Better AI responses
  • Access to all base features
Your monthly limit resets on the same day each month as your subscription date.
How does Voice Interaction work?
Voice Add-on ($1.99/month):
  • 550 interactions per month
  • 60 minutes total per month (3,600 seconds) of voice coaching
  • 30 minutes max per session
  • Real-time voice responses from your AI coach
  • Perfect for in-game coaching or hands-free learning
Free users do not have access to voice interaction. Upgrade to Pro and add the Voice Add-on to unlock this feature.
What happens if I hit my usage limit?
If you reach your daily or monthly limit, you'll see a message letting you know. Free users: Wait 24 hours for daily reset, or upgrade to Pro.
Pro users: Wait for your monthly reset, or add additional features.
Your usage counter is always visible at the bottom of the chat.

üéØ Accuracy & Fortnite Updates

How accurate is the coaching advice?
PathGen v2 is trained on competitive Fortnite strategies, pro player tactics, and current meta analysis. However, AI coaching should complement, not replace, practice and experience. The AI provides guidance based on proven strategies but may not account for every unique situation in-game.
Is PathGen updated for the current Fortnite season?
Yes! PathGen v2 is updated regularly to reflect the current Fortnite meta, weapons, map changes, and competitive strategies. We monitor patch notes and update the AI's knowledge base to ensure relevant, up-to-date advice.

Current Season: Chapter 5 Season 1 (OG Season)
Last Updated: December 2025
Can PathGen help with Zero Build mode?
Absolutely! PathGen adapts advice based on your game mode preferences:
  • Zero Build strategies (positioning, gunplay, rotations)
  • Build Mode tactics (piece control, edits, box fights)
  • Competitive ranked advice
  • Creative practice routines
Just ask and specify which mode you're playing!
Does PathGen work for controller players?
Yes! PathGen provides tailored advice for:
  • Controller players: Aim assist optimization, build binds, sensitivity
  • Keyboard & Mouse players: Keybinds, edit speed, crosshair placement
Mention your input method for the best personalized advice.

üí≥ Billing & Refunds

How does billing work?
Pro Subscription ($6.99/month): Billed monthly via Stripe. You can cancel anytime from your account settings.

Add-ons: Voice Interaction ($1.99/month), Gameplay Analysis ($1.50/month), and Competitive Insights ($0.75/month) are billed separately and can be added/removed at any time.

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We don't store your payment information.
What is your refund policy?
7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: If you're not satisfied within the first 7 days of your subscription, contact us at support@pathgen.dev for a full refund.

After 7 days: Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. We're committed to making sure you're happy with PathGen.

Usage-based refunds: If you've used less than 10% of your monthly quota and want to cancel within the first 14 days, we'll issue a prorated refund.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, absolutely! You can cancel your Pro subscription or any add-ons at any time from:
  • Settings ‚Üí Manage Subscription
  • Or through the Stripe Customer Portal
When you cancel, you'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
Do you offer discounts or student pricing?
Currently, we offer:
  • 50% off first month for new Pro subscribers
  • Special pricing for content creators and competitive teams (contact us)
We're working on student and bulk pricing options. Stay tuned!

Yes! PathGen v2 is fully responsive and works on:

Yes! Go to Settings ‚Üí Clear Chat History to delete all your conversations. You can also delete your entire account from Settings if needed.

üîß Troubleshooting

The AI isn't responding. What should I do?
Try these steps:
  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  3. Check if you've hit your usage limit (shown at bottom of chat)
  4. Try logging out and back in
  5. If the issue persists, contact support@pathgen.dev
Voice feature isn't working. Help?
Common fixes:
  • Allow microphone permissions when prompted
  • Use a supported browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox)
  • Check that you have Voice Add-on enabled (Pro users only)
  • Make sure you're not on Free tier daily limit (30 seconds/day)
  • Try using headphones to reduce background noise
I'm getting charged but can't access Pro features. Why?
This usually means your payment is still processing. Check:
  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Manage Subscription
  • Verify your payment went through in Stripe
  • Log out and log back in to refresh your account
  • Wait 5-10 minutes for our system to update
Still not working? Email us at support@pathgen.dev with your account email and we'll fix it immediately.
