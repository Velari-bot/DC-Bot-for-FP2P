Getting Started

  1. Sign Up & Choose Your Plan
    Create your free account or upgrade to Pro for unlimited coaching. Add optional features like Voice Interaction, Gameplay Analysis, or Competitive Insights.
  2. Start Chatting with Your AI Coach
    Ask questions about Fortnite gameplay, mechanics, strategy, or competitive meta. Get instant, personalized advice tailored to your playstyle and skill level.
  3. Use Voice Mode (Optional)
    Hold the microphone button to speak your questions. Get real-time voice responses with tactical coaching tips, just like having a coach in your ear during practice.
  4. Upload Replays for Analysis (Pro Feature)
    Upload your Fortnite replay files to get detailed analysis of your gameplay, identify mistakes, and receive specific improvement recommendations.
  5. Track Your Progress
    Monitor your improvement over time with usage tracking, conversation history, and personalized training recommendations based on your questions.

How Our AI Coach Works

PathGen uses advanced AI technology powered by OpenAI to provide personalized Fortnite coaching. Our system is specifically trained on Fortnite gameplay, mechanics, and competitive strategies.

What the AI knows:

  • Fortnite mechanics (piece control, edits, movement, aim)
  • Competitive meta (POIs, loadouts, storm patterns, rotations)
  • Coaching patterns (identifying mistakes, suggesting improvements)
  • Replay analysis (spotting errors, evaluating gameplay)

Important: The AI coach provides guidance and advice based on Fortnite knowledge, but cannot guarantee wins or specific outcomes. Your performance depends on practice, skill, and many factors beyond AI coaching.

Privacy & Data

Your conversations with PathGen are processed by OpenAI's API to generate responses. We store your chat history, usage data, and subscription information securely in Firebase.

What we store:

  • Your chat conversations (for history and context)
  • Usage statistics (messages sent, voice time used)
  • Subscription and payment information
  • Account information (email, username)

What we don't store:

  • Your replay files (processed and deleted after analysis)
  • Voice recordings (transcribed and deleted immediately)
  • Payment card details (handled securely by Stripe)

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
