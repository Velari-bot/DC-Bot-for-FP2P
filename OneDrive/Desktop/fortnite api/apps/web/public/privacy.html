Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025

TL;DR: We collect only what we need to provide PathGen services. We don't sell your data. You can delete your account anytime.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account with PathGen, we collect:

  • Discord information: Username, Discord ID, email (from Discord OAuth)
  • Profile data: Display name, avatar (optional)
  • Account settings: Preferences, coaching mode selection

1.2 Usage Data

To provide our service and enforce fair usage, we track:

  • Chat messages: Your questions and AI responses (stored in Firebase)
  • Usage metrics: Number of messages sent, voice minutes used, timestamps
  • Subscription info: Your plan tier, add-ons, billing status

1.3 Voice Recordings

Important: Voice recordings are used only for transcription (via OpenAI Whisper) and are not permanently stored. We keep transcripts for your chat history, but delete audio files immediately after processing.

1.4 Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information (mobile, desktop, tablet)
  • Session data and analytics (via Firebase Analytics)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide PathGen services: AI coaching, voice responses, replay analysis
  • Enforce usage limits: Track daily/monthly quotas per tier
  • Process payments: Via Stripe (we never see your card details)
  • Improve our service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Communicate with you: Service updates, billing notifications
  • Prevent abuse: Detect and block jailbreak attempts, spam, misuse

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do NOT sell your data. Period.

We share data only with trusted service providers:

  • OpenAI: Your chat messages and voice transcripts are sent to OpenAI's API for AI responses. See OpenAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase (Google): Stores your account data, chat history, and usage metrics. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Stripe: Processes payments and manages subscriptions. See Stripe Privacy.
  • Discord: For OAuth login. We only receive your username, ID, and email. See Discord Privacy.

4. Data Retention

  • Chat history: Kept indefinitely unless you delete it (Settings → Clear History)
  • Voice recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Usage logs: Kept for 12 months for billing and analytics
  • Deleted accounts: All data permanently deleted within 30 days

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Delete your data: Delete chat history or your entire account
  • Export your data: Download your chat history
  • Correct your data: Update your profile information
  • Opt-out of analytics: Disable Firebase Analytics in settings

To exercise these rights, email support@pathgen.dev or use the Settings page.

6. Security

We protect your data with:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure authentication: Discord OAuth 2.0
  • Firebase security rules: Restrict data access to authenticated users only
  • No password storage: We use OAuth, so we never see or store passwords
  • Regular security audits: Monitor for vulnerabilities

7. Cookies & Tracking

PathGen uses:

  • Essential cookies: For authentication and session management (required)
  • Analytics cookies: Firebase Analytics to improve our service (can be disabled)
  • LocalStorage: To save your preferences and chat state locally

We do NOT use third-party advertising cookies or trackers.

8. Children's Privacy

PathGen is intended for users 13 years and older (Discord's minimum age). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover we have, we'll delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of PathGen after changes means you accept the updated policy.

10. International Users

PathGen is operated in the United States. If you're accessing from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using PathGen, you consent to this transfer.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Contact us at:

  • Email: support@pathgen.dev
  • Website: pathgen.dev
Your Privacy Matters: If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please reach out. We're committed to transparency and protecting your information.
